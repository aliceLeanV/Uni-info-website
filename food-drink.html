    Food and Drink Vendors

    The University of Sheffield campus has multiple locations where you can purchase a range of food and drinks.

    The Diamond Kitchen is great for grabbing a meal on the go, from drinks; hot and cold, and a range of foods such as burritos and grilled lunches.

    Found on the ground floor of The Diamond, by the main entrance!

    Find The Diamond Kitchen

    From breakfast to lunch to even a quick snack, Jessops West cafe leaves you with many options to choose from. Offers of a handful of pastries, avocado and egg sourdough, and much more!

    Located next to the Diamond and opposite the former Henderson's factory site!

    Find Jessops West Cafe

    Head to the Arts Tower Cafe if your in need of a small oven baked meal, Baked potatos with customised toppings! In addition, cakes and pastries can be found if you are also looking for a small treat.

    Located on the ground floor of the Arts Tower!

    Find The Arts Tower Cafe

    The Wave Kitchen is the perfect place to have lunch from the range of delicious sandwiches, nachos and salads. From breakfast to lunch options, the Wave Kitchen has it all.

    Found on the ground floor of the Wave!

    Find The Wave Kitchen
